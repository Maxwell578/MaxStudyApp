1. Set Clear Goals

Define what you want to learn before you start studying to stay focused.

2. Use the Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat to stay fresh.

3. Take Notes Actively

Write summaries or questions to engage your brain better.

4. Practice Retrieval

Test yourself on what you learned without looking at your notes.

5. Stay Consistent

Short daily study sessions beat cramming before exams.
